INTRODUCTION:

Sabre utilizes multiple personas and journey maps to help drive design decisions, but we met and discovered two things, one being that there isn't a journey map covering the entire traveler journey, and that each stage in the journey involves things that the current journey maps don't have. As such, I was tasked with not only conducting research on the traveler's journey, but also creating a high level journey map and an in-depth journey map of the entire journey.

Solution

After multiple meetings, ample research, and interviews, we determined the changes and additions we wanted to make, and I then created the updated journey maps: one high level, and one detailed.
